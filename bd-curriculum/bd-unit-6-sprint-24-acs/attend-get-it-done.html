Attend Get it Done Sessions

Why Attend?

Get it Done sessions are your opportunity to work with expert instructors in real-time to prepare career artifacts that will help you land your dream job. These sessions provide personalized guidance, immediate feedback, and structured support to ensure your career materials stand out to employers.

What is a Get it Done Session?

Get it Done sessions are live experiences taught by our expert instructors designed to help you prepare the required career artifacts.

Because Get it Done sessions are structured to focus on different aspects of career artifacts, it is recommended that you come to a session having watched or read the core competency content.

Resume Workshop

Create a powerful technical resume that highlights your skills and projects. Get real-time feedback from professional resume writers.

LinkedIn Optimization

Learn how to build a professional LinkedIn profile that attracts recruiters and showcases your technical expertise.

GitHub Portfolio Review

Get feedback on your GitHub profile and projects to ensure they demonstrate your coding abilities effectively.

How to Schedule a Get it Done Session in this Unit?

In this unit, you will have access to multiple Get it Done Sessions covering all the career artifact preparation topics.

SCHEDULE GET IT DONE

Since your career artifacts will be graded in this unit, it is recommended that you attend a Get it Done session to help you finalize your career artifacts.

Get it Done Preparation Checklist:

To make the most of your Get it Done session, complete these preparation steps:

  • Watch or read the core competency content before coming to class
  • Download and make a copy of the resume template
  • Create your LinkedIn and GitHub accounts
  • Prepare questions about your specific career artifacts
  • Bring drafts of any materials you've already started

Pro Tip

Come prepared with specific questions and areas you'd like to improve. The more specific you are, the more valuable feedback you'll receive during the session.
