How to Schedule 1-1 Career Artifacts Review

Receive personalized feedback on your career artifacts through a one-on-one session with a career coach. This guide will walk you through the process of scheduling your review session.

Why Schedule a 1-1 Review?

One-on-one career artifact reviews provide personalized guidance tailored to your specific needs and career goals. These sessions offer:

  • Detailed feedback on your GitHub profile, resume, and LinkedIn
  • Customized advice for highlighting your technical skills effectively
  • Opportunity to ask specific questions about your career materials
  • Actionable steps to improve your professional presentation

Before You Schedule

To make the most of your one-on-one review, ensure your career artifacts are ready for review:

Preparation Checklist

  • Complete your GitHub profile with bio, profile picture, and pinned repositories
  • Update your resume using the BloomTech template format
  • Complete your LinkedIn profile with professional photo, summary, and experience
  • Prepare specific questions you want to ask during your review session

Not sure if your artifacts are ready? Review the Career Artifacts Submission Guide for detailed requirements.

How to Schedule Your 1-1 Review

Step-by-Step Process

  1. 1

    Login to BloomTech Portal

    Access the BloomTech portal with your credentials

  2. 2

    Navigate to Career Services

    Select the "Career Services" tab from the main navigation menu

  3. 3

    Select "Schedule a 1-1 Review"

    Click on the "Schedule a 1-1 Review" button

  4. 4

    Choose Available Time Slot

    Select from available 30-minute time slots in the calendar

  5. 5

    Submit URLs in Advance

    Provide links to your GitHub, LinkedIn, and resume for pre-review

  6. 6

    Confirm Your Appointment

    Review details and click "Confirm Appointment"

What to Expect During Your Review

Your 30-minute review session will include:

  • 5 minutes: Introduction and review of your career goals
  • 15 minutes: Detailed feedback on your career artifacts
  • 5 minutes: Your specific questions
  • 5 minutes: Action plan and next steps

Important: You will receive a written summary of your review and action items within 24 hours of your session.
