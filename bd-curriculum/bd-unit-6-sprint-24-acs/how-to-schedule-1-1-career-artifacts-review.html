How to Schedule 1-1 Career Artifacts Review

Receive personalized feedback on your career artifacts through a one-on-one session with a career coach. This guide will walk you through the process of scheduling your review session.

Why Schedule a 1-1 Review?

One-on-one career artifact reviews provide personalized guidance tailored to your specific needs and career goals.

Personalized Feedback

Detailed feedback on your GitHub profile, resume, and LinkedIn tailored to your career goals

Skill Presentation

Customized advice for highlighting your technical skills effectively to stand out to employers

Direct Questions

Opportunity to ask specific questions about your career materials and job search strategy

Action Plan

Actionable steps to improve your professional presentation and increase your hiring potential

Before You Schedule

To make the most of your one-on-one review, ensure your career artifacts are ready for review:

Preparation Checklist

  • Complete your GitHub profile with bio, profile picture, and pinned repositories showcasing your best work
  • Update your resume using the BloomTech template format with current projects and skills
  • Complete your LinkedIn profile with professional photo, summary, and experience sections filled out
  • Prepare specific questions you want to ask during your review session to maximize value

Not sure if your artifacts are ready? Review the Career Artifacts Submission Guide for detailed requirements.

How to Schedule Your 1-1 Review

Step-by-Step Process

  1. 1

    Login to BloomTech Portal

    Access the BloomTech portal with your credentials at app.bloomtech.com

  2. 2

    Navigate to Career Services

    Select the "Career Services" tab from the main navigation menu in your dashboard

  3. 3

    Select "Schedule a 1-1 Review"

    Click on the "Schedule a 1-1 Review" button in the Career Services dashboard

  4. 4

    Choose Available Time Slot

    Select from available 30-minute time slots in the calendar that fit your schedule

  5. 5

    Submit URLs in Advance

    Provide links to your GitHub, LinkedIn, and resume for pre-review by the career coach

  6. 6

    Confirm Your Appointment

    Review details and click "Confirm Appointment" to secure your time slot

  • Introduction (5 minutes): Review of your career goals and what you hope to accomplish in the tech industry
  • Detailed Feedback (15 minutes): Comprehensive review of your career artifacts with specific suggestions for improvement
  • Your Questions (5 minutes): Opportunity to ask specific questions about your materials or job search strategy
  • Action Plan (5 minutes): Development of concrete next steps to enhance your career materials

You will receive a written summary of your review and action items within 24 hours of your session to help you implement the feedback.
