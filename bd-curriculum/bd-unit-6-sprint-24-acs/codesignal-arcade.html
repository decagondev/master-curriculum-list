LeetCode: Practice & Improve

Why Use LeetCode?

LeetCode provides an engaging platform to practice your coding skills through a series of progressively challenging problems. These exercises help you build the fundamental skills needed for technical interviews and assessments while making learning structured and effective.

What is LeetCode?

LeetCode offers a variety of coding challenges that help you develop and strengthen your programming skills. The platform breaks down complex programming concepts into manageable, engaging exercises.

Key Benefits:

  • Progressive difficulty to steadily build your skills
  • Immediate feedback on your solutions
  • Points system and achievements to track progress
  • Covers essential algorithms and data structures
  • Helps prepare for technical interviews and assessments

How to Get Started

  1. Sign in to your LeetCode account
  2. Navigate to the Problem Set section
  3. Start with the Easy challenges and progress to more difficult sections
  4. Aim to complete at least 20-30 challenges before technical interviews

Pro Tip

When solving LeetCode challenges, focus on writing clean, efficient code. Don't just aim to pass the tests—try to optimize your solutions for time and space complexity, as this practice will help you perform better in technical interviews.

Recommended Practice Path

To prepare effectively for technical interviews, we recommend following this practice path:

  1. Weeks 1-2: Complete at least 15 challenges from the Easy Problems section
  2. Weeks 3-4: Work through 10-15 challenges from the Algorithm Study Plan
  3. Weeks 5-6: Take on 5-10 more advanced challenges from your choice of sections
  4. Final Week: Review challenges that gave you difficulty and refine your solutions

After completing this practice path, take a Practice Assessment to evaluate your readiness for technical interviews.
