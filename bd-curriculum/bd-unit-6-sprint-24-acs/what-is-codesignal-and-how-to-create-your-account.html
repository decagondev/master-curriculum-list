What is CodeSignal?

CodeSignal is an online skills-based assessment platform that is used for technical hiring. It is a platform that allows you to practice and get your coding skills certified.

In order to participate in the certified assessments and practice challenges, you must be set up in this environment and get familiar with the interface.

Key Benefits of CodeSignal

  • Practice coding challenges in a real-world environment
  • Get certified in your technical skills
  • Access to a wide range of programming languages
  • Prepare for technical interviews with industry-standard questions

Instructions

  1. Set up your account in CodeSignal. Use this link: Create Your Account in CodeSignal
  2. Sign up with your personal email address.

NOTE: When you create your CodeSignal account, you must use the same email address that you use to log in to the Portal - this is CRITICAL, otherwise your results will not sync to the BloomTech database.

What happens if the email I use to log in to CodeSignal to take the General Code Assessment (GCA) test differs from the email I use to log in to the Portal?

  • If the email logins don't match, your GCA test results will not be tracked in the BloomTech database, and you will have to retake the GCA using your matched login credentials.
