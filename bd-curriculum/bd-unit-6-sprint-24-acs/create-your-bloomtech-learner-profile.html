Create Your BloomTech Learner Profile

Your BloomTech Learner Profile is a professional platform that showcases your skills, projects, and achievements to potential employers. It's an essential part of your job search strategy and a powerful tool to highlight your technical expertise.

Why Create a BloomTech Learner Profile?

Professional Presence

  • Establish your online professional identity
  • Showcase your technical skills and projects
  • Highlight your BloomTech achievements

Career Advantages

  • Discovered by hiring partners through BloomTech's network
  • Complement your resume and LinkedIn profile
  • Connect with the BloomTech alumni community

Note: Your BloomTech Learner Profile must be completed before graduation to qualify for career support services.

Creating Your Profile

1
Access the Platform

Log in to the BloomTech Learner Hub and navigate to the Profile section in your dashboard.

2
Complete Your Personal Information

Add your name, professional headline, location, and contact information. Upload a professional headshot photo.

3
Write Your Professional Summary

Create a compelling bio that highlights your technical skills, career goals, and what makes you unique as a developer.

4
Add Technical Skills

List all programming languages, frameworks, tools, and methodologies you've learned at BloomTech and through personal projects.

5
Showcase Your Projects

Upload screenshots, descriptions, and links to repositories for your best projects. Highlight team projects and individual accomplishments.

6
Include Education and Certifications

Add your BloomTech education, course completion status, and any additional certifications or relevant education.

7
Link Social and Professional Profiles

Connect your GitHub, LinkedIn, portfolio website, and other relevant professional platforms.

8
Review and Publish

Carefully review all sections, check for errors or omissions, and publish your profile to make it visible to employers.

Tips for an Outstanding Profile

Content Best Practices

  • Use action verbs and quantify achievements when possible
  • Highlight problem-solving skills and technical challenges overcome
  • Be specific about technologies and methodologies used in projects
  • Ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date

Visual Presentation

  • Use a professional, high-quality headshot
  • Include screenshots or demos of your best work
  • Ensure text is well-formatted and easy to read
  • Organize information in a logical, easy-to-scan format

Pro Tip: Ask a peer or mentor to review your profile before publishing. They may spot areas for improvement or highlight strengths you've overlooked.
