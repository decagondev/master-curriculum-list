Sprint Challenge Overview

Sprint Types

  • Technical Assessment Sprints - testing your coding and problem-solving skills
  • Career Readiness Sprint - ensuring you're prepared for the job market

Keys to Success

  • Consistent daily practice and preparation throughout the sprint
  • Utilizing available resources like study guides and practice problems
  • Active participation in support sessions and study groups

Technical Sprint Requirements

Core Requirements

Assessment Score

Achieve a minimum score of 295 on the General Code Assessment (GCA) test

Required for Graduation

Technical Interview

Successfully complete the technical interview demonstrating your understanding of core concepts

Required for Graduation

Preparation Tips

  • Practice coding problems regularly using platforms like CodeSignal and LeetCode
  • Review core concepts and algorithms daily, focusing on areas where you're less confident
  • Participate in mock interviews with peers to build confidence and improve communication

Pro Tip: Set aside a specific time each day for GCA practice. Consistent small sessions are more effective than occasional cramming.

Career Readiness Requirements

Career Artifacts
