Important Information

Essential information about technical requirements, expectations, and resources for success in the program.

Technical Requirements

Required Hardware

  • Computer with minimum 1.6 GHz Processor
  • Minimum 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)
  • 120 GB Hard Drive (256 GB SSD recommended)
  • Webcam and Microphone

Supported Operating Systems

  • macOS (Recommended)
  • Windows 10 Pro
  • Ubuntu Linux

Note: Chromebooks are not supported as coursework requires local software installation.

Program Expectations

Attendance and Participation

Success in the program is measured through multiple components:

  • Active participation in live instruction sessions
  • Completion of module projects and sprint challenges
  • Regular engagement with learning materials
  • Consistent progress through the curriculum

Activity Requirements

Students who are inactive for more than seven (7) consecutive days will be contacted to verify enrollment status.

Leave of Absence

If you are unable to maintain engagement due to extenuating circumstances, you may need to request a leave of absence. Contact your advisor to discuss your options.
