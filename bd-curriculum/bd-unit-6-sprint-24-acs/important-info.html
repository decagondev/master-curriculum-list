Technical Requirements

Required Hardware

  • Computer with minimum 1.6 GHz Processor
  • Minimum 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)
  • 120 GB Hard Drive (256 GB SSD recommended)
  • Webcam and Microphone for live sessions

Supported Operating Systems

  • macOS (Recommended)
  • Windows 10 Pro
  • Ubuntu Linux

Important Note

Chromebooks are not supported as coursework requires local software installation.

Program Expectations

Attendance and Participation

Success in the program is measured through multiple components:

  • Active participation in live instruction sessions and discussions
  • Completion of module projects and sprint challenges on time
  • Regular engagement with learning materials and resources
  • Consistent progress through the curriculum at the expected pace
View Detailed Expectations

Activity Requirements

Students who are inactive for more than seven (7) consecutive days will be contacted to verify enrollment status. Continued inactivity may affect your standing in the program.

Leave of Absence

If you are unable to maintain engagement due to extenuating circumstances, you may need to request a leave of absence. Contact your advisor to discuss your options.

A leave of absence can help protect your progress and enrollment status when you're dealing with unexpected life events.
