Important Note

Your BloomTech Learner Profile will be a key element in your job search success. It serves as your professional showcase to potential employers and your connection to the BloomTech hiring network.

Creating Your BloomTech Learner Profile

Follow these steps to create a compelling BloomTech Learner Profile that effectively showcases your skills and projects to potential employers.

Step 1: Access the Profile Builder

Navigate to the BloomTech Portal and find the "Learner Profile" section in your dashboard.

https://profiles.bloomtech.com/login

Step 2: Complete Basic Information

  • Add a professional headshot photo
  • Provide your preferred name and contact information
  • Craft a compelling headline that highlights your tech focus
  • Write a professional summary showcasing your skills and goals

Step 3: Showcase Your Technical Skills

Add your technical skills organized by category:

Example Skills Section

Languages

  • JavaScript
  • Python
  • HTML/CSS
  • SQL

Frameworks & Libraries

  • React
  • Node.js
  • Express
  • Redux

Step 4: Add Your Projects

Include 3-5 of your best projects with the following information:

  • Project title and brief description
  • Technologies used
  • Your role and contributions
  • Links to live demo and GitHub repository
  • Screenshot or preview image

Step 5: Connect Your Profiles

Link all your professional profiles:

  • GitHub account
  • LinkedIn profile
  • Personal portfolio website
  • Blog or Medium account

Step 6: Set Job Preferences

Specify your job search parameters:

  • Desired roles (e.g., Frontend Developer, Full Stack Engineer)
  • Location preferences or remote options
  • Salary expectations
  • Work arrangement (full-time, contract, etc.)

Profile Submission Process

Once you've completed your BloomTech Learner Profile, follow these steps to submit it for review:

  1. Ensure all sections are completed with detailed information
  2. Preview your profile to check for any errors or improvements
  3. Submit your profile through the BloomTech Portal
  4. Schedule a profile review session with a career coach
  5. Incorporate feedback and make necessary adjustments

Important Deadline

Your BloomTech Learner Profile should be completed by the end of your second sprint to ensure maximum visibility to hiring partners.
