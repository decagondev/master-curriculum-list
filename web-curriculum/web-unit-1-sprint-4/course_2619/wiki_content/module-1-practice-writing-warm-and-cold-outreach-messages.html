Networking is the most successful way of finding a meaningful job and attaining career success.  In fact, did you know that 80% of open positions are filled through networking? 

You now have an opportunity to practice what you learned in this module!  

To help you improve your networking and outreach skills, in this practice activity, you will write a warm and cold outreach message.

Instructions:

1. In your personal Google drive or on your desktop, create two new folders one for Warm Outreach and one for Cold Outreach. This is where you will save your outreach messages

2.  Prepare 1 warm outreach message.  Your warm network are individuals whom you already know. They could be alumni, past colleagues, former managers, or friends. Follow the best practice tips that you learned from the video lesson.

  • Make a list of 5 individuals in your network. 
  • Out of this list, select 1 individual that you would want to reach out to first
  • Create a new Google Doc and draft 1 outreach message to this selected individual.

3. Prepare 1 cold outreach message.  Your cold network are individuals that you do not know. They could be LinkedIn contacts or a referral through someone you know. 

  • Identify 1 individual that you do not know and would like to reach out to.  
  • Draft 1 “cold” outreach message to the selected individual in a Google doc. 

For this activity, you only have to practice writing 1 warm and 1 cold outreach. The intent of this is to help you get started!  As you learned in this module, it is important to make a plan on how you will continue with your networking and outreach ...as this should be an ongoing activity!
