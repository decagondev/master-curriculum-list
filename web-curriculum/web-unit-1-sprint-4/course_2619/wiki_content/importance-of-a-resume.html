Introduction

Since your resume serves as your first impression on potential employers it is extremely important to ensure it aligns with the roles you are applying for and highlights the skills the employer is looking for. We will ensure that by the time you complete BloomTech, your resume is ready to be put in front of employers. To get started we are going to discuss two main topics:

  • Resumes Through the Eyes of a Tech Recruiter

Since tech recruiters are often the first eyes that will ever view your resume you want to keep them in mind as you create and build content. 

  • ATS Systems

These automated systems act as gatekeepers, sifting through countless resumes to identify candidates that match specific criteria set by employers. However, navigating through ATS can be a challenge, as they rely on algorithms to parse and analyze resumes. Understanding how ATS works and optimizing your resume accordingly is essential.
