LinkedIn Profile Basics

Getting Started with LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a powerful platform to showcase your skills, experience, and professional brand. Let's start by setting up the basics of your profile.

Initial Setup Steps:

  1. Edit your personal information (name, pronouns, etc.)
  2. Create a compelling headline with your target job titles
  3. Upload a professional profile photo
  4. Customize your profile URL
  5. Configure "Open to Work" settings
