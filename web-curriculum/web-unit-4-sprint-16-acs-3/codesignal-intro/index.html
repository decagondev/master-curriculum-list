Introduction to CodeSignal

What is CodeSignal?

CodeSignal is an online skills-based assessment platform used for technical hiring. It allows you to:

  • Practice coding challenges
  • Get your coding skills certified
  • Prepare for technical interviews
  • Track your progress

Setting Up Your Account

1

Create Your Account

Visit CodeSignal Signup to create your account.

2

Use Your Portal Email

Sign up using the same email address you use to log into the Portal.

Important Note

Using a different email address than your Portal email will result in:

  • GCA test results not syncing to the BloomTech database
  • Need to retake the GCA with correct credentials

CodeSignal Arcade

The Arcade is your practice playground in CodeSignal. Here you can:

  • Solve coding challenges at your own pace
  • Practice specific programming concepts
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Prepare for the GCA test

Introduction to GCA

What is the General Code Assessment (GCA)?

The GCA is a standardized coding assessment that:

  • Evaluates your programming skills
  • Measures your problem-solving abilities
  • Assesses your coding efficiency
  • Provides detailed feedback on your performance

Practice Tips

  • Start with Arcade challenges
  • Take practice GCA tests
  • Review your solutions
  • Focus on time management
