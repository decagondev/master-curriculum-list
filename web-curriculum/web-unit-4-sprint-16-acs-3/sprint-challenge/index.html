Sprint Challenge - Web Unit 4 Sprint 16 ACS 3

Sprint Challenge Overview

Final Assessment

This sprint challenge is your final assessment for Web Unit 4 Sprint 16. It consists of two main components:

Career Readiness

Submit your updated LinkedIn profile and resume to demonstrate your professional development.

Technical Assessment

Complete the General Code Assessment (GCA) to showcase your technical skills.

Challenge Requirements

Career Readiness Requirements

LinkedIn Profile

  • Professional profile photo
  • Customized headline with target job titles
  • About section with your story and skills
  • Education section with BloomTech details
  • Experience section with previous positions
  • Skills section with at least 50 relevant skills
  • Projects section showcasing your work
  • Open to Work settings configured

Resume

  • Updated with current skills and experience
  • Projects section with technical details
  • Education section with BloomTech program
  • Professional formatting and layout

Technical Requirements

General Code Assessment (GCA)

  • Complete the official GCA test
  • Score must meet minimum requirements
  • Multiple attempts allowed

Submission Instructions

How to Submit

  1. Update your LinkedIn profile with all required sections
  2. Finalize your resume with current information
  3. Take the General Code Assessment (GCA)
  4. Submit the following links:
    • Your LinkedIn profile URL
    • Your updated resume link
    • A job description for a role aligned with your skills
