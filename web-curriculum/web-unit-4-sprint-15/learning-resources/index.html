Important Information

Tech Requirements

Required:

  • A Computer with:
    • 1.6 GHz Processor
    • 4 GB RAM
    • 120 GB Hard Drive
    • Web Cam
    • Microphone
  • Required Downloads, Installations, and Account setups

Recommended:

  • 2 GHz Processor
  • 8 GB RAM
  • 256 GB Hard Drive (preferably solid state)

Operating Systems (in order):

  • macOS
  • Windows 10 Pro
  • Ubuntu
  • Other Windows versions (may have occasional issues)

Chromebooks are not supported

Unit Overview

Engagement Expectations

Attendance and participation are measured through multiple methods, including live instruction attendance, module & sprint project attempts, and LMS activity.

Learners must maintain activity in the Learning Management System. Inactivity for more than seven (7) days will result in being flagged as inactive.

BloomTech may withdraw inactive learners to protect them from unintentional further vesting of tuition.

Learners facing extenuating circumstances may need to take a leave of absence.

How to Pass This Sprint

Technical Sprint Requirements:

  • Receive 100% on the Sprint Challenge (unlimited attempts)
  • Receive 80% on the Sprint Assessment (unlimited attempts)

ACS Sprint Requirements:

  • Attempt the official General Code Assessment (GCA)
  • Complete the corresponding career readiness assignment
