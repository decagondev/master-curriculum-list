What is a Get it Done Session?

Get it Done sessions are live experiences taught by our expert instructors designed to help you prepare the required career artifacts.

Because Get it Done sessions are structured to focus on different aspects of career artifacts, it is recommended that you come to a session having watched or read the core competency content.

Session Structure & Benefits

What to Expect

  • Interactive, hands-on format
  • Small group setting for personalized attention
  • Real-time feedback from industry experts
  • Structured activities to complete artifacts
  • Peer review opportunities

Session Outcomes

  • Completed or significantly improved artifacts
  • Clear action items for any remaining work
  • Understanding of industry expectations
  • Confidence in presenting your professional materials
  • Network with peers and instructors

Get it Done Preparation Checklist

  • Did you watch or read the core competency content before coming to class?

    Review related materials in the curriculum to maximize your session productivity.

  • Did you download and make a copy of the resume template?

    Having the template ready will allow you to focus on content during the session.

  • Did you create your LinkedIn and GitHub accounts?

    Create these accounts before the session so you can focus on optimizing rather than setting up.

  • Have you gathered information about your projects?

    Be ready to discuss: technologies used, your role, challenges overcome, and outcomes.
