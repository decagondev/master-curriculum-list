There is only one tried and true way to get better at solving the types of technical coding challenges that you will encounter during interviews and will face while trying to land your first technical job: PRACTICE!

A great way to start practicing is to use the CodeSignal Arcade 

What is CodeSignal Arcade? It is a collection of problems that you can use to help you improve your problem-solving and coding skills. If you can solve 50-60 problems, this will help you to achieve a quality score on the General Code Assessment (GCA)

Practice as often as you can - even 10 minutes per day or one challenge per day will help you get the necessary amount of exposure and practice needed.
