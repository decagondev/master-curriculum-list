Overview

Now that you have completed the practice activities, you are ready to take the practice General Code Assessment (GCA) test. You should take this test as often as permitted.

The more you practice and feel comfortable taking this type of test, the better chance you have to achieve a score of 650 or higher.

Instructions

  1. Login to CodeSignal
  2. Click on this link to take the practice GCA HERE
  3. Once logged into CodeSignal, select your preferred language and click NEXT
  4. Agree to the Terms and Pledges. Click START
  5. Complete the questions. Click on FINISH TEST

For your reference

  • You should have already created your Code Signal account. If you have not done so yet, please follow these instructions What is CodeSignal and How to Create Your Account
