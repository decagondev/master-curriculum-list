BloomTech offers a variety of Live Class options. Please take a moment to review our offerings and schedule the ones that work best for you.

Sprint Reviews

Sprint Reviews 1-2 are 30-minute live experiences taught by instructors that are targeted to help you grasp major concepts found in the sprint challenges.

  • Sprint Review 1 will cover a concept in the first two modules;
  • Sprint Review 2 will cover a concept in the last two modules.

Ask an Expert

Ask an Expert sessions are 30-minute live experiences staffed by an instructor. Are you stuck on a module and just can't seem to connect the dots? Have a question, and the online searches are getting you nowhere? These are purposefully designed to get you unblocked and unstuck in the BloomTech curriculum.

Code-Alongs

Code-Alongs are 50-minute live experiences taught by our expert instructors designed to prepare you for concepts found in the sprint challenges. Code-Alongs are your opportunity to work on complex job-ready problems in a live and engaging environment. You may see them in the Portal later on in your track. Because Code-Alongs delve deeper into a core competency, you will need to come to class prepared to have the best experience.
