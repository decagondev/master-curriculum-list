What is the GCA Requirement in this Unit?

During the core ACS units, you were introduced to the technical assessment preparation content, completed practice activities, and attempted the General Code Assessment (GCA).  

In this unit, you must attempt the official General Code Assessment test and also achieve a minimum score of 295 to pass this unit. 

**VERY IMPORTANT**

You MUST create your CodeSignal account using the SAME email address you use to login to the learning portal. Failure to do so will mean that your GCA test scores will not sync to the BloomTech database. 

What happens if the email I use to log in to CodeSignal to take the General Code Assessment (GCA) test is different than the email I use to log in to the Portal?

If the email logins don't match, your GCA test results will not be tracked in the BloomTech database, and you will have to retake the GCA using your matched login credentials.

Please reference these pages before you continue:

  • What is CodeSignal and How to Create Your Account
  • CodeSignal User Interface Overview
  • Introduction to GCA

Instructions:

  1. You will take the official GCA and submit the test in CodeSignal.
  2. Login to CodeSignal* using the same email address you use to login to the portal.
  3. Take the official GCA HERE
  4. After you submit the test in CodeSignal, it will take 1-3 business days to receive your official score
  5. Once you receive an email from CodeSignal with your official score, Enter the score in the Text Box below
  6. Check the Performance Page in the BloomTech Portal to ensure your score is registered.

Note:  it will take up to 1-3 business days to receive your official GCA score; you must remember to return to this sprint challenge page and enter the GCA score once you have received your official score.  Failure to do so will prevent you from passing and moving on to the next unit.

What happens if I do not achieve a score of 295?

You can retake the GCA; however, remember that there is a 2-week cool-off period before you can retake the test.  After you retake the GCA, follow the same steps above under Instructions.

Do I need to retake the GCA if I already have 295?

No, if you already have 295, you do not need to retake the GCA, but you are still required to enter the GCA score of that test into the Text Box below.

I achieved a score of 295, but I want to see if I can score higher

Yes! While you may have achieved the minimum score requirement, you are encouraged to retake the GCA and improve your score - can you get a 712 or higher? Aim for the highest possible score you can achieve!  

After the two-week cool-off period from your last GCA attempt, follow the instructions above to retake the GCA.

What if I Retake the GCA But End up with a Lower Score

Not to worry! We keep track of your highest score on the back end, so just know as long as you submit an official GCA score of a completed test, your highest score will be tracked.
