Overview

It is time to finalize your career artifacts and submit them!

During the core units, we provided you with the tools (core competency videos and practice activities) to help you build your career artifacts.  

Instructions

Fill out the required fields on the Submit Your Career Artifact Links form

  • You will be prompted to provide:
    • GitHub URL
    • LinkedIn URL
    • Resume URL **must use the BloomTech resume template and remember to set sharing permission to “anyone with the link” and grant editor access**

What Happens Next

After you submit your career artifacts, a reviewer will provide written feedback. Please allow up to 3 business days to receive this feedback. Feedback will be in the form of an email and SMS text. Please pay close attention to your inbox during this time.

Based on the feedback you receive, you have the opportunity to make the necessary changes to your career artifacts and resubmit using the form above (see Instructions). 

To move to the next unit, you must receive a passing grade in your career artifacts.
