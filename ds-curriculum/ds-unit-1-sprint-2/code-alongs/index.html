Code-Alongs

Introduction

Code-Alongs are live, interactive sessions where you'll work on practical problems alongside an expert instructor. These 50-minute sessions are offered multiple times throughout the week and will help reinforce key concepts from your modules while preparing you for the sprint challenge.

What Are Code-Alongs?

Key Information

  • Live 50-minute sessions with expert instructors
  • Focused on practical application of sprint concepts
  • Available multiple times throughout the week
  • Designed to prepare you for concepts in the sprint challenge
  • Opportunity to work on complex job-ready problems

Available Code-Along Sessions

Code-Along 1: T-Tests

This code-along focuses on practical applications of T-Tests, reinforcing concepts from Module 1. You'll work through realistic examples, applying your knowledge of hypothesis testing with T-Tests to analyze data and draw conclusions.
