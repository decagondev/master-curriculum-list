Unit 4 Data Science

Unit Description:

Unit 4 focuses on advanced data processing and Machine Learning techniques, primary Natural Language Processing (NLP), Neural Networks, and Deep Learning. These powerful tools will supercharge your modeling skillset and give you the ability to process new kinds of data including text documents, images, and time series data.

What you will bring to a job after this course

Job Skills
Sprint 13
  • Use Python string methods and Regex where necessary to clean text data
  • Break text into clean tokens in preparation for vectorization
  • Use and judge between a variety of Vectorization Techniques for taking unstructured text data and turning it into a structured numeric representation
  • Use LSA and LDA for Topic modeling. 
  • Use Word Embeddings and Transfer learning to process text data
Sprint 14
  • Understand the structure of fully-connected neural networks and how they are trained
  • Use your understanding of how Neural Networks are tuned to develop intuition for appropiate network achitecture, hyperparameters, and hyperparameter tuning
  • Use Keras to model and train a Multi-Layer Perceptron Neural Network for basic supervised learning tasks (regression or classification)
Sprint 15
  • Understand what "Deep Learning" means (and its limitations) so that it becomes more than a buzzword for you. 
  • Use Convolutional Neural Networks, (convolutional layers) to process image data with a neural networks
  • Use Recurrent Neural Networks to process data that has a sequence or recency bias to the training data.
  • Use LSTMs (A kind of RNN) to supercharge your use of RNNs
  • Use Auto-encoders to clean, compress, or denoise data.
  • Use LSTMs to forecast future time periods based on historical patterns.
