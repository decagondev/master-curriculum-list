Career Artifacts Review Overview

Understanding the 1-1 Review Process

The 1-1 career artifacts review process provides you with direct feedback from career coaches to enhance your professional materials. These personalized sessions help you refine your resume, GitHub profile, and LinkedIn profile to stand out to potential employers.

Benefits of 1-1 Reviews:

  • Personalized feedback tailored to your career goals
  • Industry-specific recommendations for your field
  • Immediate clarification on improvement areas
  • Actionable strategies to enhance your professional brand
  • Guidance on highlighting your unique value proposition

Learning Objectives

What You'll Learn

  • How to prepare for your 1-1 review session
  • Key elements of an effective career portfolio
  • Common feedback areas and how to address them
  • Post-review action steps for improvement

Skills You'll Develop

  • Creating a professional brand that aligns with industry standards
  • Effectively showcasing technical projects and contributions
  • Crafting compelling professional narratives
  • Implementing feedback to iterate on career materials

The Review Process

1

Preparation

Before your 1-1 review session, ensure you have:

  • Updated your resume with the BloomTech template
  • Completed your GitHub profile with projects and README files
  • Updated your LinkedIn profile with education and skills
  • Prepared specific questions about your materials
2

During the Review

What to expect and how to make the most of your session:

  • Be ready to share your career artifacts with the coach
  • Take notes on specific feedback points
  • Ask clarifying questions when needed
  • Discuss industry-specific customizations
3

Implementation

After your review, take action on the feedback:

  • Prioritize revisions based on coach recommendations
  • Make specific improvements to each artifact
  • Apply consistent branding across all materials
  • Develop a timeline for completing all updates
4

Follow-Up

Continuing improvement after initial feedback:

  • Schedule a follow-up review if needed
  • Seek peer feedback on your revised materials
  • Test your materials with industry professionals
  • Continue to update as you develop new skills
