Sprint Challenge for DS Unit 3 Sprint 12

Overview

The Sprint Challenge for DS Unit 3 Sprint 12 consists of two main components:

  1. A coding assessment that tests your knowledge of objects, caching, and linked lists
  2. A career readiness component that requires you to submit your completed resume

This challenge is designed to assess your understanding of the core competencies covered in this sprint.

Instructions

Part 1: Coding Assessment

For the coding component, you will need to:

  • Log into your CodeSignal account
  • Complete the assigned Sprint Challenge assessment
  • The assessment will include questions related to:
    • Using dictionaries and objects to solve problems
    • Implementing caching strategies to optimize code
    • Working with linked lists and object references
    • Understanding Big O notation and algorithm efficiency
  • You will have a time limit to complete the assessment

Part 2: Resume Submission

For the career readiness component, you will need to:

  • Finalize your resume using the BloomTech resume template
  • Ensure all sections are completed as instructed in Module 4
  • Submit your resume in PDF format

Submission

To complete the Sprint Challenge, you must submit both components:

  1. Complete the CodeSignal assessment by the deadline
  2. Submit your resume PDF to the designated submission portal

Both components must be submitted to receive credit for the Sprint Challenge.
