Code-Alongs

Overview

Code-Alongs are live experiences taught by expert instructors designed to prepare you for concepts found in the sprint challenges. These sessions offer the opportunity to work on complex job-ready problems in a live and engaging environment.

Code-Alongs are 50 minutes in length and are offered seven days a week in the morning, afternoon, and evening. They provide deeper insights into your core competencies and help reinforce your understanding of the sprint material.

Preparing for Code-Alongs

The best Code-Along experiences happen when you come prepared. Your instructors have created starting points and solutions for each Code-Along to ensure you have what you need to succeed.

Ideal Code-Along Preparation Checklist:

  • Review the core competencies before coming to class
  • Watch the guided projects before coming to class
  • Take the checks for understanding before coming to class
  • Finish your module projects before coming to class

What to Bring:

  • Your laptop with a working Python environment
  • A LeetCode account (create one if you haven't already)
  • Questions about concepts you're struggling with
  • A willingness to participate and collaborate
