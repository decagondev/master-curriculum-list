Sprint Challenge

Complete the following requirements to successfully complete this sprint challenge.

Resume Submission

Submit Your Professional Resume

Ensure your resume is properly formatted and includes:

  • Professional heading with contact information
  • Skills section highlighting technical and data science capabilities
  • Education section including your BloomTech training
  • Project descriptions that showcase your technical abilities

Your resume should follow the template provided in Module 4 and demonstrate your understanding of effective resume writing principles covered in the career readiness modules.

Technical Coding Assessment

Complete LeetCode Problems

Complete the following LeetCode problems to demonstrate your understanding of:

  • Array manipulation and numerical operations
  • Conditional logic and branching
  • String handling and pattern recognition
  • Algorithm design and optimization

Required LeetCode Problems:

  • Product of Array Except Self - Array manipulation and numeric operations (Medium difficulty, builds on Module 1 concepts)
  • Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters - String processing and conditional logic (Medium difficulty, builds on Module 2 concepts)
  • Rotate Array - Array manipulation and algorithmic thinking (Medium difficulty, builds on Module 3 concepts)
  • Group Anagrams - String manipulation and data structure organization (Medium difficulty, combines concepts from Modules 2 and 3)

Complete at least 3 of these problems and submit links to your solutions or screenshots showing your completed work.

General Code Assessment (GCA)

Complete the Official GCA Test

As part of this sprint challenge, you are required to attempt the official General Code Assessment (GCA) test. This standardized assessment evaluates your coding skills and provides a score that reflects your programming proficiency.

For this sprint, you must attempt the GCA test. While there is no specific score requirement to pass the sprint, you should aim to achieve a score of 295 or higher. This target score will become a requirement later in the program.

Taking the GCA:

  • Create a CodeSignal account using the same email address you use to login to the BloomTech portal
  • Take the official GCA at this link: BloomTech GCA Test
  • Record your score in the assignment submission

Important Information:

  • The GCA consists of 4 programming tasks to complete in 70 minutes
  • Only your highest score is tracked in the BloomTech database
  • You can achieve a score of 295 by getting 100% on tests and passing any task (Task #1 is generally the easiest)

Next Steps

After Completing the Sprint Challenge

Review your next steps and prepare for upcoming content:

  • Review feedback on your resume and make necessary improvements
  • Continue practicing LeetCode problems to strengthen your coding skills
  • Analyze your GCA results to identify areas for improvement
  • Begin networking using the outreach strategies covered in Module 4
  • Prepare for the next sprint on advanced data science concepts

Grading Rubric

Your Sprint Challenge grade is based on the following criteria:

  • Resume Submission (33%): Successfully submitting a professionally formatted resume
  • LeetCode Problems (33%): Completing at least 3 of the required LeetCode problems
  • GCA Attempt (34%): Taking the CodeSignal GCA test and submitting your score

Note: While achieving a specific GCA score is not required for this sprint, attempting the GCA test is mandatory. Your highest GCA score will be tracked for future requirements, where a score of 295 or higher may be needed.
